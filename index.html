About

Founded by industry leaders with extensive experience in electronics and semiconductor technology, Single Photon consultancy draws from a strong legacy of innovation and market leadership. Originating from the successful commercialization of cutting-edge sensor technologies, including Single Photon Avalanche Diodes (SPAD) and Silicon Photomultipliers, the company has a proven track record in high-volume manufacturability and scaling advanced technologies for global markets. Our founding team originated in SensL. turning it from a Ph.D project into a market leader, achieving significant sales growth and establishing a robust, multi-country supply chain. SensL's success across multiple sectors, including its profitable acquisition by onsemi in 2018, reflects the expertise we bring to our clients today. We continue to leverage this knowledge, helping businesses navigate complex challenges, optimize operations, and achieve sustainable growth.
